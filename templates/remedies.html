General Poultry Care Best Practices

Housing Management
  • Provide adequate space (2-3 sq ft per bird)
  • Ensure proper ventilation and temperature control
  • Maintain clean and dry bedding
  • Regular cleaning and disinfection
Nutrition & Feeding
  • Provide balanced commercial feed
  • Ensure constant access to clean water
  • Supplement with greens and grains
  • Provide grit for digestion
Biosecurity
  • Limit visitor access to poultry areas
  • Use foot baths and disinfectants
  • Control wild bird and rodent access
  • Quarantine new birds for 2-3 weeks
Health Monitoring
  • Daily observation of bird behavior
  • Regular weight checks
  • Monitor feed and water consumption
  • Keep vaccination records updated

Emergency Contact

For immediate assistance with sick poultry, contact your local veterinarian or agricultural extension officer.

Remember: Early detection and professional intervention are crucial for successful treatment of poultry diseases.
